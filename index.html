A Few Words About UsWelcome to Guru Gobind Singh Vidya Mandir

Guru Gobind Singh Vidya Mandir Sr. Sec. School Ratwara Sahib was established by His Holiness Sant Baba Waryam Singh Ji Maharaj and Honorable Mata Ranjit Kaur Ji. It was inaugurated on 8th April,2001. It is an English medium Co-educational school affiliated to CBSE, New Delhi. School has huge four-storey building in the lap of nature. School campus is spread in 10 acres with lush green lawns and having spacious play grounds. The main aim of the school is to uplift the rural children and to provide excellent educational facilities and modern equipments to facilitate learning process. School is accountable to foster superior human values in students to develop dynamic personalities to make them professionals and respectable citizens of our country.

In Punjab, it is running 123 formal schools and more than 341 informal educational institutions (Sanskar Kenders).
